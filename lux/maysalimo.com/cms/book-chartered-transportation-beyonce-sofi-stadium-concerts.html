Transportation Woes at Beyoncé's SoFi Stadium Concerts

Beyoncé is bringing her highly anticipated Renaissance World Tour to Los Angeles in early September, with shows scheduled at SoFi Stadium on September 1, 2 and 4, 2023. These concerts are poised to be major events, with tens of thousands of Queen Bey fans descending on Inglewood. However, getting to and from SoFi Stadium presents some major transportation challenges.

The Challenges of Getting To and From SoFi Stadium

SoFi Stadium was built without direct public transit access. The nearest metro stations are a couple miles away, requiring shuttles or buses for the last leg of the journey.

With so many people coming and going in a short window around Beyoncé's set times, traffic congestion before and after the shows will be extreme.

Demand for rideshares like Uber and Lyft will likely lead to surge pricing and long wait times.

Parking at SoFi Stadium and nearby lots is limited and will fill up quickly. Parking costs are also expensive, ranging from $50-100+.

While public transit can help avoid traffic, capacity is limited and transfers are required. Based on past major events at SoFi, buses may face delays from gridlock.
