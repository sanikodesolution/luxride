Leave Game Day Driving to Us: Take Maysa Limo to Rams Games

As the official home stadium of the Los Angeles Rams, SoFi Stadium will host exciting football action all season long. But as any fan knows, getting to and from the stadium on game days can be a headache. This season, leave the driving hassles behind and ride with Maysa Limo.

Avoid the Gridlocked Traffic and Parking Nightmare

SoFi Stadium was built for massive events, but the surrounding infrastructure wasn't. That means traffic getting to Rams games is jam-packed. And with limited parking, finding a spot is tough.

After the game ends, getting out is even worse. You'll sit in gridlock for hours trying to exit the parking lot and nearby streets. It's frustrating and exhausting.

Ride Relaxed All the Way to the Big Game

With Maysa Limo, skip the traffic and parking frustrations. Our chartered buses and limos pick you up and drop you off right at SoFi Stadium’s entrance.

Enjoy comfortable, luxurious rides in our fleet of vehicles equipped with premium amenities and features. Sit back and ride relaxed knowing our experienced drivers handle navigation.

Customize Your Game Day Experience

Not only do we provide transportation, we offer a full experience. When chartering one of our vehicles, you can customize your ride with:

  • Catered food and drinks
  • Entertainment like TVs and music
  • Rams themes and decor
  • Goodie bags for your group

Part of the fun is tailgating en route! Our chartered buses and limos become an extension of the pre/post-game party.

Reserve Game Day Charters Today

Get in touch now to reserve your chartered transportation for upcoming Rams games. Our fleet books up fast, so don’t delay.

Secure your spot and arrive at SoFi Stadium relaxed, refreshed and ready to cheer on the Rams!
